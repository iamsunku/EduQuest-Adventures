Program Objectives

  • Enhance students' learning experience through hands-on activities.
  • Foster curiosity, teamwork, and problem-solving skills.
  • Develop social, emotional, and cultural awareness.
  • Provide exploration, discovery, and fun opportunities.

Trip Schedule

Trips are planned for every public holiday, including Sundays (approx. 15-20 trips per year), avoiding exam periods.

Destination Categories

  • Science and Technology (e.g., Planetarium, Science museums)
  • History and Culture (e.g., Monuments, Cultural festivals)
  • Environmental and Nature (e.g., National parks, Botanical gardens)
  • Art and Architecture (e.g., Art galleries, Architectural landmarks)
  • Social and Community Service (e.g., Orphanages, Community service projects)
